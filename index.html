About Meddy's Africana Buka

At Meddy's Africana Buka, we bring you the authentic taste of Africa combined with international favorites. Our passion is to serve delicious, home-style African cuisine that reminds you of home while introducing new flavors to adventurous food lovers.

With three convenient locations across Lagos, we're committed to providing exceptional food quality, warm hospitality, and a dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Africa.
